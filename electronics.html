Why yes, I MacGyver electronics; how could you tell?

Time and time again, consumer electronics disappoint me in their complete lack of product serviceability and lack of available spare parts for the consumer. We are supposed to chuck the entire product in the garbage, for the failure of a single part. I am morally opposed to this annoying habit of rapid consumer cycle, and try to repair my stuff. Besides the projects mentioned above, I have also dabbled in building digital and analog amps, guitar pedals, and a bench power supply from an ATX psu.
