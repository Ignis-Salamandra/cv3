Beer brewing adventures

I was begrudgingly taken by 2 friends to a beer making workshop. Thought it was going to be a rotten evening where I paid out of pocket, for the dubious trade of hours of physical labor for my very own mediocre beer. Boy, I couldn't have been more wrong. Ever since then I have made beer, with a particular focus on old beer such as the recipe interpretations of "A Hymn to Ninkasi". One of these days I'll make a dedicated website for my beer experiments.
